National Parks and Campgrounds Finder

Use this search tool to find the National Parks within a state you'd like to visit. Add an optional search term to narrow down your findings. When you find a park you'd like to explore, click on the "Show Campgrounds" button below each park. A list of campgrounds for that park will display. Make your selection, pack your hiking boots & gear, and get ready to enjoy a socially distanced National Parks getaway!

Before you go, refer to this CDC resource for guidelines and restrictions when visiting parks and camping facilities.
