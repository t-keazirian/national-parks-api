National Parks and Campgrounds Finder

Do you want to get away during quarantine? 2020 has been rough, and exploring the great outdoors might be just what you need!

Get your hiking boots and gear. Pack up for a socially distanced National Parks getaway! Refer to this CDC resource for guidelines and restrictions when visiting parks and camping facilities.

Use this search tool to find the National Parks within a state you'd like to visit. If you see a park that strikes your fancy, you're all set! Feel free to add an optional search term to narrow down your findings.

Once you find a park you'd like to explore, just click on the button that says "Show Campgrounds" to pull up a list of campgrounds available for that park.
