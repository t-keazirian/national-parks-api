National Park and Campgrounds Finder

Do you want to get away during quarantine? 2020 has been tough, but exploring the great outdoors might be just what you need!

Get your hiking boots and camping gear, and pack up for a socially distanced National Parks Getaway!

Use this search tool to find the National Parks within a state you'd like to visit. If you see a park that strikes your fancy, you're all set! Feel free to add an optional search term to narrow down your findings.

If you're the camping type, once you find a park you'd like to explore, just click on the button that says "Show Campgrounds" to pull up a list of campgrounds available for that park.
